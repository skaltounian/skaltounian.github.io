Vor Zanarad Pazoogs Ko

Vor zanarad pazoogǝs ko i khachin daradzetser Kristos Asdǝvadz, yev nǝshan haghtootyan yedoor mez; sovav ǝzgyanǝs mer bahya.

Vor zashkharhagetsooyts khachi ko Kristos Asdǝvadz kavazan zorootyan yedoor mez; sovav ǝzgyanǝs mer bahya.

Vor ǝzmeryaln i jashagmane paydin hantsanats, genats paydivn genakordzetser; sovav ǝzgyanǝs mer bahya.

Your Immaculate Arms

Your immaculate arms, O Christ God, You stretched on the cross, And you gave us a symbol of victory; through your cross protect our lives.

You stretched your arms on the cross, You saved the world, O Christ God, and you gave us a staff of strength; through your cross protect our lives.

Tasting from the tree of sin we inherited death, you saved us through the tree of life; through your cross protect our lives.
